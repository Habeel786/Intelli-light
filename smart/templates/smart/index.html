What is Intelli-light?

Intelli-light is a smart street light concept which works on proximity of vehicle with the poles

How it works?

As it was stated earlier, it is seen in a number of cities that the street light is one of the huge expenses in a city. The cost spent is huge that all the sodium vapor lamps consume more power. The expense spent on the street light can be used for other development of the nation

we made a concept of smart street light system which will detect the vehicle coming towards and with help of advance algorithm it will turn on the front lights for a certain distance and will turn off the lights which were passed by the vehicle.

this system is applicable for the paths which are not so busy but due to lack of street lights there are chances of collision but at same time if lights are installed there will be much waste of energy due to less frequency of vehicles.
